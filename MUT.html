Missionary Upholders Trust (MUT) is an umbrella body for Mission
organizations with a Vision and Mission to meet common unmet needs of over 22,000 missionaries and pastors from member missions and member pastoral associations serving all over India.

The needs are met through various interventions in the physical, emotional, spiritual and financial areas of their life. One of the main areas of MUT is to provide immediate support in times of crisis


  • Medical assistance - To the affected member
  • Bereavement relief - Financial support to the family of deceased


Present Challenge
The second wave of Covid19 has been more destructive than the first wave and hospitalization and fatalities of members have increased. (From 3 deaths to 35 deaths per month)

Immediate Need
(As on June 2021 – more than 500 members affected and these numbers are expected to increase) Need - 3.5 crore (Bereavement relief 2cr and Medical Support 1.5cr)

Our Intervention
A free Virtual Musical Concert “Music for Missions” was broadcast on 19th June 2021 on Zoom and YouTube, to bring awareness about MUT and the present need due to Pandemic.
